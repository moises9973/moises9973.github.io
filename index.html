Hollow Knight Silksong

Hollow Knight Silksong is a game by Team Cherry that just released last week and I have been having a lot of fun. It is a 2D platformer with a focus on boss fights. Even though it is 2D, exploring the map is still fun, and the combat is also fun because of how cool the movement is. It is really hard, but that makes it more fun. Its good to finally play this game after waiting 7 years after it was announced.
